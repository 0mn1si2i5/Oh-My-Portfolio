## What I noticed

Small, opinionated ideas often reveal more about product-making than a long requirements document: where curiosity begins, what deserves polish, and when an experience feels complete enough to share.

## What I explored

NBTI was an independent space to turn a compact premise into something people could directly try, without treating experimentation as a lesser version of product work.

## What I made

I designed and built NBTI as a public web experience, from its initial concept through implementation and deployment. It remains deliberately lightweight: a focused interaction instead of an oversized platform.

## What I learned

The project reinforced a simple practice I value: make the smallest complete thing that lets an idea meet a real audience, then learn from that encounter.
