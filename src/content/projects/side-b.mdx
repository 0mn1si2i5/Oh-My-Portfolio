## What I noticed

Sharing a song should be a small social gesture. Instead, the link can send the recipient to the wrong platform, strip away useful context, or turn a recommendation into a dead end.

## What I explored

I explored what a platform-agnostic sharing experience could feel like: a link that first understands the song, then preserves the momentum of the recommendation across different listening habits.

## What I made

I designed and built the core “parse — gather — share” flow as a SwiftUI iOS prototype with a FastAPI service behind it. The system resolves Spotify, Apple Music, NetEase Cloud Music, and QQ Music inputs into a canonical song object, then supports local saving, platform routing, and room-based discussion with persisted messages and live updates.

The screens shown here use real resolver output with a curated local listening room: a song detail view makes the available destinations legible, while the room keeps the song card, reply, and reactions in the same social context.

## What I learned

The project remains a prototype, but building the working path made the cost of a wrong match concrete. A platform button only feels useful when the system can earn the user's trust in the song it points to. That became the guiding constraint: show real destinations when they exist, and keep uncertainty visible instead of presenting a guess as a success.

<section lang="zh-CN" data-locale-content="zh">

## 我注意到的问题

跨平台分享音乐时，链接可能无法在接收方常用的平台直接打开，也容易丢失歌曲信息和分享语境，降低一条推荐真正被听到的可能性。

## 我探索的方向

我探索一种平台无关的分享体验：先识别歌曲，再根据不同的听歌习惯提供可用去向，并保留推荐本身的社交语境。

## 我做出的东西

我将“解析 — 汇集 — 分享”实现为 SwiftUI iOS 原型，并以 FastAPI 服务提供支持。系统将 Spotify、Apple Music、网易云音乐和 QQ 音乐的输入统一解析为标准歌曲对象，随后提供本地收藏、平台跳转，以及具备持久消息和实时更新的房间讨论。

页面展示真实的解析结果与本地听歌房间。歌曲详情页清晰呈现可用平台；房间页则将歌曲卡片、回复和反应置于同一讨论上下文中。

## 我学到的事

项目仍处于原型阶段，但完整实现后可以明确看到错误匹配的成本。平台跳转只有在歌曲识别可信时才有价值，因此系统的核心约束是：存在可靠去向时再展示；存在不确定性时如实呈现，而不将猜测包装为成功结果。

</section>
