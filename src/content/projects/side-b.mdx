## Why sharing a song still breaks down

Your friend listens on NetEase; you use Spotify. Most of the time, sharing becomes sending a title and asking the other person to search again. That is merely annoying until it is a track in another language, a live cut, or a reissue—then it is easy to land on the wrong version. A small recommendation can stall before either person hits play.

## How Side B handles it

Side B accepts Spotify, Apple Music, NetEase Cloud Music, and QQ Music links, then resolves them into one track object before showing the available destinations. The point is not to create another player; it is to remove the guesswork between receiving a recommendation and hearing the intended song.

## What I built

I built the core “parse — gather — share” flow as a SwiftUI iOS prototype with a FastAPI service behind it. The app keeps recent resolutions and personal playlists locally, routes people to their preferred platforms, and lets a room hold song cards, replies, and reactions in one place.

The screens shown here use real resolver output and a curated local listening room. They show both entry points: resolving a link for yourself and resolving it directly in the conversation where it was shared.

## What I would test next

Side B also raises a harder product question: will people install another app just to share a song? I would test two different answers. One is to make the resolver almost invisible—bring it to a mini-program or web handoff, then send people straight to the service they already use. The other is to make the destination worth opening—recurring rooms, shared collections, and lightweight community features can give recommendations a place to accumulate. The first reduces the cost of a single use; the second gives people a reason to return.

## 为什么分享一首歌，常常还是很麻烦

朋友用网易云，你用 Spotify。大多数时候，分享一首歌最后会变成：他把歌名发给你，你再自己搜一遍。平时只是多几步；碰上小语种、现场版或重制版，就很容易搜到另一首。原本一句“你听听这个”，可能还没开始播放就断在了中间。

## Side B 怎么处理这件事

Side B 接住 Spotify、Apple Music、网易云音乐和 QQ 音乐的链接，先把它们识别成同一首歌，再展示可用的去向。它不是想再做一个播放器，而是想把“收到推荐”和“听到对方说的那一首”之间的猜测去掉。

## 我做了什么

我把“解析 — 汇集 — 分享”的核心流程做成了 SwiftUI iOS 原型，后端由 FastAPI 支撑。App 会在本地保留最近解析和个人歌单，支持跳转到常用平台；房间里则把歌曲卡片、回复和表情回应放在同一处。

这里展示的是实际解析结果和一个本地策展的听歌房间：既可以为自己解析一条链接，也可以直接在分享它的对话里完成解析。

## 下一步要验证什么

但 Side B 也绕不开一个更现实的问题：为了分享一首歌，用户为什么要多装一个 App？下一步我会验证两种不同的回答。第一种是把解析能力做得几乎无感：迁到小程序或网页，用更顺的跳转把人直接带回常用平台。第二种是把房间做成值得再打开的地方：让共同收藏、主题房间和熟人互动慢慢积累起来。前者降低单次使用的成本，后者给用户一个留下来的理由。
