<section lang="en" data-locale-content="en">

## What we noticed

An LLM conversation starts out feeling light: ask, answer, continue. But as a task develops, it becomes a long hallway. Alternatives disappear behind the newest message; returning to an earlier decision means scrolling, reconstructing context, and hoping the right thread is still there.

## What we explored

Li Ran and I asked a simple question: what if a conversation behaved less like a transcript and more like a workspace? We treated revisitation as a first-class interaction—something people should be able to see, navigate, compare, and branch from without first rebuilding the past in their heads.

## What we made

DialogTree is the product expression of that idea. It turns a conversation into an interactive tree: follow a path, fork from any node, mark what matters, and retrieve earlier material through text or semantic search. Underneath are a D3-based workspace, streaming chat, persistent history, and retrieval-aware search; on the surface, the goal is simply to make a long conversation feel inhabitable again.

## From DialogTree to Branchat

The product also became the research prototype for _Branchat: A Tree-Structured Interface for Efficient Revisitation in Long-Horizon LLM Conversations_, an ACM CHI 2026 Poster co-authored by Jiaming Li, Peinuan Qin, and Yi-Chieh Lee. We designed information-revisitation tasks, instrumented the prototype, and ran a 12-participant within-subject study with a Latin-square A/B design. Across complex retrieval tasks, the tree interface supported faster completion and a lower reported cognitive burden—evidence that interface structure can change how people keep hold of context while working with an LLM.

<section lang="zh-CN" data-locale-content="zh">

## 我们观察到的问题

随着 LLM 对话不断延长，线性聊天界面会使早期讨论逐渐被新消息淹没。用户若要回看某个决策、比较不同思路或复用既有上下文，往往只能反复滚动并手动重建对话脉络。

## 我们探索的方向

我和李然关注的是：能否将对话从线性记录转化为可导航的知识结构。重点不只是展示更多消息，而是让用户能够定位上下文、比较分支，并从任意节点继续展开新的探索。

## 我们实现的产品

DialogTree 将对话组织为可交互的树状结构。用户可以沿路径浏览、从任意节点创建分支、标注关键节点，并通过文本或语义检索找回历史内容。产品结合 D3 树状工作区、流式聊天、持久化对话记录和检索感知搜索，用于支持长对话中的回溯与管理。

## 从 DialogTree 到 Branchat

2026 年，DialogTree 的核心交互发展为研究原型 _Branchat: A Tree-Structured Interface for Efficient Revisitation in Long-Horizon LLM Conversations_。该工作由 Jiaming Li、Peinuan Qin 和 Yi-Chieh Lee 共同完成，并以 ACM CHI 2026 海报论文形式发表。我们围绕信息回溯设计实验任务，在原型中记录交互行为，并以拉丁方 A/B 设计完成 12 人组内实验。结果显示，在需要反复检索和整合信息的复杂任务中，树状界面能够提升完成效率，并降低参与者报告的认知负担。
